Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Vanilla ice cream is a timeless frozen dessert renowned for its velvety smooth texture, pale creamy-white color, and rich, sweet flavor with delicate undertones of vanilla. Crafted from a luscious blend of cream, milk, sugar, and either vanilla beans or extract, it offers a luxurious mouthfeel and a warm, floral aroma that tantalizes the senses. Whether enjoyed solo, as a complement to various desserts, or adorned with an array of toppings and mix-ins, vanilla ice cream's versatility knows no bounds, making it a beloved indulgence for people of all ages and occasions worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 100g of white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Making the base: Cook the milk, cream, and sugar until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Making the base Pt. 2: Transfer the mixture to a measuring cup, then add the vanilla extract. Chill overnight.
  3. Turning the base into ice cream: Pour the mix into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's directions.
  4. Serving: Serve immediately or ripen in the freezer before serving.
